SEO files in this project
=========================

**robots.txt** — Tells search engines what to crawl. Blocks `/admin/` and `/api/`
from indexing. Upload next to `index.html`.

**sitemap.xml** — List of important public URLs for Google / Bing. Upload next to
`index.html`.

Before you go live
------------------
1. Pick your canonical site URL (must be `https://`, e.g. `https://yoursite.com`).
2. In **both** `robots.txt` and `sitemap.xml`, replace every occurrence of  
   **`https://CHANGEME.invalid`** with that URL (no trailing slash on the origin
   when replacing the hostname part; paths like `/booking.html` stay as they are).
3. In Google Search Console (and Bing Webmaster), submit your sitemap URL:  
   `https://YOUR-DOMAIN/sitemap.xml`

Optional: add your real domain to Open Graph / JSON-LD in `index.html` if you add
those tags later.
